Secure Shopping

We realize that you may have concerns about placing an order online and sending us your credit card number over the Internet. For your security, orders placed on our site are encrypted using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology, which ensures that your transaction is protected. The information you send to us via the Internet can be trusted to arrive privately and unaltered. Please note that e-mail is not encrypted and is not considered a secure means of transmitting credit card numbers. If you would like to learn more about Secure Shopping on our website, see below. Or, if you have any questions regarding Secure Shopping on our website, please contact us.

Your credit card number is secure

When you use your credit card to shop our Site, rest assured that the transaction is secure. Our servers use (TLS), an advanced encryption technology that works with Google Chrome (version 40 and above), Microsoft Internet Explorer (version 11.0 and above), Microsoft Edge, Firefox (version 35 and above), Safari (version 8.0 and above). Note: You should always update your browsers to the latest versions to mitigate vulnerabilities. This technology safeguards your personal information and guarantees privacy.

How to check the security of your browser

To check the security of your connection, look at the lower left- or right-hand corner of your browser window after accessing the server. If you see an unbroken key or a closed lock (depending on your browser), then SSL is active and the page you are accessing is being transmitted by SSL. You can also double-check by looking at the URL line of your browser. When accessing a secure page, the first characters of the site address will change from "http" to "https."

Some versions of browsers and some firewalls don't permit communication through secure servers. In that case, you won't be able to connect to the server and, therefore, won't be able to place an order through an insecure connection. If you cannot access the secure server for any reason, please call us TOLL-FREE at 1-800-798-2921 to place your order.

If you still feel more comfortable ordering over the phone, please call us TOLL-FREE at 1-800-798-2921. One of our Customer Service Representatives will be happy to help you.

About cookies and how we use them

A cookie is a small file that a Website puts on your computer's hard drive so that it can remember you at a later time. This file contains nothing more than a 32-digit code. It does not contain your name, address, or any other personal information. Our cookies do not identify an individual user specifically, such as by name or address.

We use cookies for two reasons. (1) Cookies allow us to retain any items you've placed in your shopping basket, should you decide to leave your shopping basket and view other areas on our site, or other websites. If you do this, when you are done and return to your shopping basket, the items you selected are still there for you to buy. (2) Cookies also tell us whether you have visited neutonpower.com previously, and help us determine whether you came to our website from a particular Internet link or banner advertisement.

It's important to note that the data we gather via cookies is analyzed in aggregate. This means we analyze the trends established by thousands of visitors through our site. We do not analyze data on the individual customer level.

Your Rights Under the California Consumer Privacy Act

The California Consumer Privacy Act states residents of the state of California have the right to:

  • Request the personal information collected about them
  • Request the personal information collected about them be deleted
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information

The categories and types of information collected are listed in detail above in our privacy policy.

Making a request

  • In order to request disclosure, deletion, or opt-out sale or sharing personal information, please contact us using the form on the contact us page, or call our contact center at 1-800-798-2921.
